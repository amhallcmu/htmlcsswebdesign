This is a fish I caught in, oh, about 2002 off the coast of Tunis.

You can't see it from the picture, but the fish was 600 pounds and 45 feet long. I broke two rods trying to catch it.

Of course, this made national news. In Tunisia, I was dubbed "The Girl of 10,000 Lucky Fins." This was translated from an Arabic proverb that, unfortunately, loses some of its delicacy in English.
